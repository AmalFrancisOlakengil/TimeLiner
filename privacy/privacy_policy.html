Privacy Policy for TimeLiner

Last Updated: June 19, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how TimeLiner handles your information.

1. Data Collection

TimeLiner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Your usage of the application is private and stays on your device.

2. Local Storage

TimeLiner functions as a desktop application. All data created within the application, including your schedules and events, is stored exclusively in a local database (SQLite) on your computer. We do not have access to this data, and it is not uploaded to any cloud services or remote servers.

3. Permissions

The application requires "Full Trust" permissions solely to manage your local data files. These permissions are not used to access your personal accounts, internet traffic, or other sensitive system information.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

5. Contact Us
